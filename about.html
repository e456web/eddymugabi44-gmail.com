About Finova

Our mission, vision, and the team behind the innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Finova began as a small team of financial experts and technologists with a shared vision to disrupt traditional banking systems. Today, we're a leading fintech innovator serving clients in over 20 countries.

Our Mission

To democratize financial services through technology, making banking more accessible, efficient, and secure for businesses and individuals alike.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing boundaries in financial technology
  • Integrity: Transparent and ethical in all our operations
  • Customer Focus: Solutions designed with real user needs in mind
  • Collaboration: Partnering with clients for mutual success
